About Us

Agencja Horyzont has been involved in professional wedding videography and creating wedding reports since 2002, when its founder Radosław Zawadzki graduated from Camerimage Film School (former branch of Łódź Film School).

His experience gained during the production of television reports by editor Leszek Ciechonski for TVP2's Express Reporters program, allowed him to develop skills and experience in reportage filming where, as we know, you need to be fully concentrated and ready to act in various circumstances. We specialize in wedding filming and creating wedding films at the highest level.
